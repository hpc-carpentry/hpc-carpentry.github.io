The Carpentries Trainer community comprises a group of experienced Instructors who inspire and prepare the next wave of Instructors in our community. They work as a team to maintain and teach the instructor training curriculum to prepare prospective Carpentry Instructors to join the community. They showcase and embody the enthusiasm and conduct of our community as they prepare new Instructors. Becoming a Trainer lets you scale your impact - sharing your own passion, experience, and enthusiasm with the next generation of Carpentry Instructors. Trainers meet regularly. They also have their own dedicated Slack channel and email list. Trainers can expect to acquire this skill set. Find out more about becoming a Trainer.
